Our Profile

Pangea aims to connect Brands from 7 continents around the world by acting as Principal Agency to help conquer local market challenges. As we are living in a connected world through technology, people might feel connected with the same brand and enjoy the same experience.

Our Vision

To help International Brands who are looking to develop the International Market with Localization strategy. We serve as extended arms to build localization strategy ranging from legal & requirement study, product selection, brand development, marketing efforts, sales management and after sales responsibilities.

Our Mission

To help International Brands grow further from its domestic roots using a combination of Digital & Traditional strategy. We use the latest Commerce and E-commerce development to make direct connections with our Customers, Retailers, Dealers, or Distributors to maximize growth potential.

What We Do?

We help international brands expand by adapting their identity to local preferences

  • We help International who are looking to develop into outside territory. We want to replicate.

  • We work exclusively to transfer brand identity to be understood by the local market.

  • We help International brands to have Local operations in the most efficient & effective way.

  • We help International Brand to tailor made products according to local presence.

Why Work With Us?

Maximize numerous opportunities in the local market by partnering with us

Local team knowledge to navigate local market

Having a local team provides valuable insights and an understanding of market ups and downs. This includes knowledge of local consumer behaviors, cultural nuances, and regulatory requirements.

Save cost through actual budget vs GMV

Focusing on the actual budget rather than Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) helps maintain financial discipline and efficiency. Ensure that every budget spending is aligned with achieving specific business goals, reducing wastage.

Two-way communications

We are willing to listen to Brand’s original idea to be preserved and transferred to the local market. Our team will work hand in hand to match business strategy together.

Platform and Media Management

We build our own platform to support Online and Offline business as well as communication channels with our customers & communities.

Online -Offline presence focus

We use external platforms; social media, E - Commerce and live streams to support and grow further offline sales.

Enhance Brand Visibility and Market Penetration

Effectively increasing brand visibility and penetration in the local market to establish a strong market presence.
